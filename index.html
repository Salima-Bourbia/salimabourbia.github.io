About me

I am a PhD student at Mohammed V University in Rabat, Morocco and ISEN Yncréa Ouest/Nantes in Carquefou, France. My research interests include 3D computer vision, machine/deep learning, and mainly the evaluation of the quality of 3D point clouds.
